# Demo Walkthrough

This guide explains how the two normal Demo professions can clear
`Demo: 灰烬哨站的封匣` without using the cheat role.

Audience:

- players who want a stable first clear
- reviewers who want to verify profession differentiation
- agents who need a fast manual route through the Demo pack

This is not a full field-by-field story spec.  
For story structure and acceptance coverage, read `README.md` in the same folder.

## What Counts As A Clear

For Demo, these endings all count as successful clears:

- `击溃守卫带走封匣`
- `逼对方让路`
- `拖到窗口完全打开`
- `立即带匣撤离`

They represent different tones of success rather than a single binary win state.

## Route 1: Scout Clear

Best for:

- fast first completion
- low-risk showcase run
- seeing how light control and stealth bonuses work together

Recommended ending target:

- `带着代价撤离`
- or a quick control-oriented finish if the encounter opens kindly

### Core idea

The scout wins by making `light` drop early and then repeatedly converting
high `agility + 潜行` into safe objective progress.

### Suggested sequence

1. `借月光复核路线`
   - this is worth doing once
   - success gives you `炭笔路线图`
2. `沿崩塌外墙潜入`
   - this is the scout's natural entry
   - on success it lowers `light` before the encounter really starts
3. `掐灭残灯`
   - now `light` should be very low or zero
4. `沿阴影贴近封匣`
   - this is your main profession-aligned action
5. if you already have `借掩警戒`, look for `借掩突进`
   - this is the clearest Demo-only example of a status-gated action
   - it also shows that an active状态 can lower the DC of a later check
6. repeat `沿阴影贴近封匣` if the board still looks safe
7. choose one finish:
   - `立即带匣撤离`
   - or another visible exit if you already have comfortable control

### Why this works

- the scout has strong `agility`
- the scout also has the strongest `潜行` rank in Demo
- the profession bonus favors `stealth` and `analysis`
- low `light` grants extra help to `agility` / `insight`
- this route often clears the Demo in very few turns

### Safety tips

- if you got `炭笔路线图`, remember it can be used before a key stealth play
- do not stay too long just because the early turns feel easy
- if `alarm` starts climbing and you already have enough objective progress, leave

### What a good scout run looks like

- low turn count
- low HP loss
- little or no corruption
- minimal item expenditure

## Route 2: Warden Clear

Best for:

- players who want a steadier brawl
- seeing contest, damage, healing, and cover interplay
- a more complete “systems showcase” run

Recommended ending target:

- `拖到窗口完全打开`
- or `击溃守卫带走封匣` if the pressure stays manageable

### Core idea

The warden wins by absorbing pressure better than the scout, then using
`力量 + 压制`, `contest`, `damage`, healing, and cover management to survive long enough for
a heavier finish.

### Suggested sequence

1. `借月光复核路线`
   - do it once
   - treat any success as a bonus, not as something to farm repeatedly
2. either:
   - `沿崩塌外墙潜入` if you want a calmer opening
   - `踹开侧门直接闯入` if you want a direct systems-heavy run
3. once inside, stabilize first:
   - `使用烟幕罐` if available
   - `翻进断墙后` when incoming physical pressure is rising
4. then start taking ground:
   - `正面夺匣`
   - `斩断守卫阵线`
5. if you gained `借掩警戒`, consider `借掩突进`
   - this is a good way to see one of the clearest `V2.0` additions in action:
   - the move is unavailable before the status exists
   - then becomes available and easier while the status is active
6. if your HP dips, use:
   - `贴墙包扎`
   - `使用急救包`
7. finish according to board state:
   - choose `拖到窗口完全打开` if the fight is messy but under control
   - choose `击溃守卫带走封匣` if enemy HP and objective pressure are already favorable

### Why this works

- the warden has better `might`
- the warden also brings stronger `坚忍` and `压制`
- the profession bonus helps `contest`
- higher max HP makes prolonged pressure less punishing
- smoke, cover, and healing let the warden convert a rough entry into a stable win

### Safety tips

- do not ignore `灰呛`; over many turns it quietly eats the run
- if `alarm` reaches the dangerous range, pure brute force gets much worse
- use `斩断守卫阵线` when you want to move toward a defeat-style ending
- use `正面夺匣` when you want objective control more than raw enemy damage

### What a good warden run looks like

- moderate turn count
- visible HP loss but no collapse
- one or two meaningful consumable uses
- either a delay finish or a hard victory finish

## Common Mistakes

### Skipping the skill-test segment when you want to validate the build

The new pre-mission skill segment is there for a reason.

- it lets you explicitly test all four Demo skills before the main encounter
- it grants small but real setup advantages
- it is the fastest manual way to confirm `attribute + skill` resolution is rendering correctly

### Repeating `借月光复核路线` too many times

It is a useful opener, but it is not meant to be farmed forever.

- failure raises `doom`
- repeated retries can make the setup phase feel worse than the actual mission

Do it once. If it succeeds, great. If not, move on.

### Staying in the encounter after the board already turned in your favor

Demo is partly about choosing how to end a mission, not only how to dominate it.

- scout players often should leave earlier
- warden players often should stabilize before pushing for more

### Ignoring environment

`light`, `cover`, and `alarm` matter a lot.

- scout loves low `light`
- warden appreciates real `cover`
- high `alarm` makes the encounter nastier than it first appears

## Quick Recommendation

If you are new to Demo:

1. play `斥候` first for a clean stealth-flavored clear
2. then play `守望者` for the heavier pressure-management route

That pair gives the clearest picture of what the current lite TRPG system is
trying to support.
